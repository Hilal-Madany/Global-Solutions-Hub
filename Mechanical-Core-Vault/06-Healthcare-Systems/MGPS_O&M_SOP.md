# Medical Gas Standard Operating Procedures (SOP)

### Phase 1: The LOX Source (Liquid Oxygen)
1. **Daily Telemetry:** Log tank pressure (10-15 bar) and level percentage.
2. **Expansion Monitoring:** Maintain 1:860 liquid-to-gas ratio logic.
3. **Vaporizer De-Icing:** Shift-wise inspection to ensure gas discharge temp is ambient.

### Phase 2: Medical Air & Vacuum Plant
1. **Dew Point Check:** Ensure desiccant dryers maintain **-40°C**. 
2. **Filtration:** Check 4-stage filters for oil carryover or particulate saturation.
3. **Lead/Lag Logic:** Verify vacuum pumps alternate to ensure even wear and constant -500mmHg pressure.

### Phase 3: Distribution (AVSU & Bedside)
1. **Zonal Isolation:** Monthly exercise of Area Valve Service Units (AVSU).
2. **Terminal Integrity:** Check gas specificity and static pressure (4 bar/60 psi).
