## ❄️ Industrial & Commercial HVAC: Concepts & Sizing
Beyond comfort cooling, we focus on **Process Cooling** and **Air Quality Management** for large-scale facilities.

### **1. Core Concepts**
* **Psychrometrics:** Analyzing Humidity ($RH$) and Dry Bulb Temperature ($DBT$) for optimal environment control.
* **Ventilation Logic:** IAQ (Indoor Air Quality) standards and pressurized ducting for dust prevention in manufacturing.
* **Heat Recovery:** Utilizing HRWs (Heat Recovery Wheels) to reduce chiller load by up to 30%.

### **2. Technical Sizing & Calculations**
* **Heat Load Estimation:** $Q = U \times A \times \Delta T$ (Calculating solar gain, occupancy, and equipment "Machine Load").
* **Air Flow (CFM):** $CFM = \frac{Sensible Heat (Btu/hr)}{1.08 \times \Delta T}$
* **Pump & Fan Laws:** Calculating static pressure ($wg$) and BHP requirements for high-static AHUs.

## 📊 Commercial Framework: BOM & BOQ
I bridge the gap between **Engineering Design** and **Commercial Execution**.

### **🛠️ BOM (Bill of Materials) - Technical**
The granular list of raw components required for assembly/installation:
* **HVAC:** Compressors, AHU Coils, VFDs, Expansion Valves, Dampers, Sensors.
* **Fabrication:** Specific tonnages of **IS 2062** steel, welding electrodes, and fasteners.

### **📝 BOQ (Bill of Quantities) - Commercial**
The quantified line items for tendering and client billing:
* **Tonnage of Ducting:** (GI Sheets per SMACNA gauge).
* **Area of Insulation:** (Nitrile Rubber/Glasswool in Sqm).
* **Equipment Units:** (Chiller TR, AHU CFM, Pump Flow GPM).
* **Piping:** (Copper/MS piping in Running Meters).
