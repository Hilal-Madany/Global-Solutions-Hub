# 🏭 Industrial Product Applications

* **Material Grade:** IS 2062 Grade A.
* **Fabrication:** 3mm MS thickness with MIG/Arc welding and Epoxy coating.
* **Principle:** Sizing based on velocity method for industrial airflow (12-18 m/s).
