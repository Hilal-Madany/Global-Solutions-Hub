## ⚙️ 5. Technical Observations & SME Definitions
During the inspection, the following critical engineering steps were performed:

* **TP Parting (Test Piece Parting):** A section was cut from the mother forging for destructive testing. This ensures the lab sample has undergone the exact same heat treatment as the final asset.
* **Proof Machining:** Forgings were released for rough machining to verify internal geometry and expose any sub-surface anomalies before final finishing.
